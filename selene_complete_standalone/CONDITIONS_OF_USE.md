## Required Caveats

### Minimum Disclosure (All Uses)

Any presentation or document referencing Project Selene ABM outputs must include:

> *"These results are from exploratory scenario analysis and reflect model behavior under specified assumptions. They should not be interpreted as predictions of real-world outcomes. External validation is pending."*

### Extended Disclosure (External Audiences)

For audiences outside the immediate project team, add:

> *"The model has been calibrated to historical cases but has not undergone out-of-sample predictive testing. Confidence intervals reflect parametric uncertainty only; structural and real-world uncertainties are not quantified."*
